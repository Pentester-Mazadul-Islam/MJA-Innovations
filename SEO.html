What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website’s visibility on search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. By optimizing your website structure, content, and backlinks, SEO ensures that your business ranks higher for relevant search queries.

SEO involves:
• On-page Optimization (content, headings, meta tags, images)
• Off-page Optimization (backlinks, guest posting, social signals)
• Technical SEO (site speed, mobile-friendliness, indexing)
• Local SEO (targeting local customers for increased footfall)
• Keyword Research & Strategy
• Analytics & Performance Tracking
