Penetration Testing (VAPT).

SERVICES / Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Identify Vulnerabilities Before Hackers Do – Secure Your Digital Assets with Professional VAPT

In an era of increasing cyber threats, protecting your website, applications, network, and sensitive data is no longer optional — it’s essential. Penetration Testing (VAPT) — Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing — is a simulated cyber attack performed by ethical hackers to discover security weaknesses in your systems before real attackers can exploit them.

Our expert VAPT services help businesses proactively detect vulnerabilities, assess risks, and strengthen their security posture, ensuring compliance and safeguarding customer trust.

Why Penetration Testing (VAPT) is Critical in 2026

• Rising Cyber Attacks: Ransomware, data breaches, and sophisticated hacking attempts are increasing every year.

• Regulatory Compliance: Meet requirements of GDPR, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, and other industry standards.

• Protect Reputation: Prevent costly data breaches that can damage your brand and customer confidence.

• Risk Mitigation: Identify and fix vulnerabilities before they lead to financial loss or legal consequences.

• Build Customer Trust: Demonstrate your commitment to security with regular security testing.

We provide manual and automated penetration testing across multiple domains:

Our Professional VAPT Methodology

1. Reconnaissance: Collecting intelligence about your systems.

2. Vulnerability Scanning: Automated scanning using industry-leading tools.

3. Manual Penetration Testing: Deep manual exploitation by certified ethical hackers.

4. Exploitation & Post-Exploitation: Simulating attacker behavior to assess impact.

5. Risk Assessment: Ranking vulnerabilities by severity (CVSS scoring).

6. Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive report with Proof-of-Concept and remediation steps.

7. Retesting & Verification: Free retesting after fixes to ensure vulnerabilities are resolved.
