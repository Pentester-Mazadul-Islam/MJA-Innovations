What is Penetration Testing (VAPT)?

Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) is a structured approach to evaluate the security of your systems, networks, and applications.

• Vulnerability Assessment: Identifies potential security weaknesses in your digital infrastructure.
• Penetration Testing: Simulates real-world cyber attacks to test how your systems respond to exploitation attempts.

VAPT helps businesses strengthen security posture, prevent data breaches, and comply with industry standards.

FAQs

Q: What is the difference between vulnerability assessment and penetration testing?
A: Vulnerability assessment identifies potential weaknesses, while penetration testing actively exploits them to check real-world impact.

Q: How often should VAPT be performed?
A: For optimal security, VAPT is recommended at least annually or after major system changes.

Q: Can you test cloud and SaaS applications?
A: Yes, our experts conduct penetration testing on web, mobile, cloud, and SaaS applications.

Q: Will VAPT disrupt my systems?
A: Our tests are conducted in a controlled and safe manner to avoid business disruption.
