What is Security Audit & Compliance?

Security Audit is a comprehensive evaluation of your IT infrastructure, applications, and processes to identify potential security gaps or vulnerabilities.
Compliance ensures your business adheres to legal, regulatory, and industry standards such as GDPR, HIPAA, ISO, and PCI DSS.

Together, these services help businesses:
• Identify and mitigate security risks
• Prevent data breaches and cyber threats
• Maintain customer trust and regulatory compliance
• Strengthen overall IT governance and risk management

FAQs

Q: What is the difference between a security audit and compliance check?
A: A security audit identifies vulnerabilities and risks, while compliance ensures your business meets legal and industry standards.

Q: How often should security audits be conducted?
A: At least annually or after major system changes to maintain security and compliance.

Q: Do you provide recommendations for remediation?
