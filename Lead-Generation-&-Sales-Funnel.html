What is Lead Generation & Sales Funnel?

Lead Generation is the process of attracting and capturing potential customers interested in your products or services.
Sales Funnel is the step-by-step journey that guides a lead from awareness to purchase, ensuring maximum conversion at each stage.

A successful lead generation and sales funnel strategy includes:
• Awareness: Reaching new potential customers through marketing channels
• Interest: Engaging leads with valuable content and offers
• Decision: Guiding leads to choose your product or service
• Action: Converting leads into paying customers
• Retention: Nurturing existing customers for repeat business

By integrating both processes, businesses can create predictable revenue streams and improve ROI.
